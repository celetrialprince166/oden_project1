Caprese Salad with Pesto Sauce

Nothing like a fresh tomato salad in summers! A great antipasto bite to start your meal with. This combination of juicy tomatoes and mozzarella cheese salad topped with freshly made pesto sauce is a distinct yet simple one. It offers a twist to the classic caprese salad.

  • For Salad:
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • Handfu Tulsi
  • 1 packet Buffalo Mozzarella
  • For Pesto Sauce:
  • Handful Basil
  • 1 cup Pine nuts
  • 1 cup Parmesan Cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup Extra virgin olive oil
  • Juice of half a Lemon

How to Make Caprese Salad with Pesto Sauce

  1. Cut 2 juicy tomatoes in thick rounds and dress them with a little bit of extra virgin olive oil , some salt and pepper.
  2. Break Mozzarella into chunks
  3. On a serving platter, arrange the tomato slices along with the mozzarella chunks.
  4. Garnish with tulsi and pesto sauce.
